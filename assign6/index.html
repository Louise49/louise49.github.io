Introductions

Main dubbing groups in China are as follows, including 729 Voice Studio, TrioPen Studio, Bianjiang Studio, Guan Sheng, Voice Bear, Voicegem, Sound-U, Listen and Shun Produce.



Members

Not only are core members of each group playing vital roles in Chinese dubbing, but they have been in an effort to introduce more young blood to the emerging industry.

Famous Works

Notices:
  • The members of these groups cooperate a lot.
  • Often, the group where the dubbing director belongs gets the title.
  • In fact, animations, radio dramas and vomics are preferred in that they have more freedom to act out.
  • It is gratifying that the dubbing cast began to appear in more and more TV series and movies.

Activities

There are various activities such as training camps, lives, CV offline meetings (usually following certain anime conventions) and CV stage shows. Sometimes, recruitment information would be announced through their official accounts.

Attention!
  • Some campaigns are launched in a joint way like stage shows.
  • Usually, training camps are divided into different levels. (for amateurs or professionals)
  • Some dubbing actors/actresses may start "voice streamings" through personal accounts on "irregular" basis. (like Kilakila)
